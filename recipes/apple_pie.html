Apple Pie

This is my famous apple pie recipe.

Ingredients

Crust

  • all-purpose flour
  • butter
  • salt
  • vodka, freezer temperature
  • ice water

Filling

  • 6 cooking apples, peeled and cut into 2 cm pieces
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4th cup honey
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/8th tsp nutmeg
  • 1/8th tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch, plus enough water to make slurry

Instructions

Crust

  1. In a large bowl, using a pastry blender, combine the butter and flour
  2. Mix the chilled vodka and ice water
  3. Slowly add vodka and water mixture little by little, until the dough sticks just sticks together
  4. Shape the dough into a ball separate into two equal parts
  5. Flatten the dough balls into disks, and wrap in clingwrap; refridgerate

Filling

  1. Over medium heat, cook the apples, butter, and sugar until just softened
  2. Add honey, spices, and salt and continue to cook
  3. Combine cornstarch with just enough water to make a slurry
  4. Add slurry to the applie filling mixture, and cook until thickened
  5. Remove from heat

Assemble the Pie

  1. Roll out the two pie crust discs into 1 cm thick sheets
  2. Place one sheet in pie pan and shape
  3. Place pie weights on top of crust
  4. Bake crust for 20 minutes
  5. Remove crust from oven, and remove pie weights
  6. Spoon filling onto crust and place second pie crust sheet over
  7. Trim extra dough from edges and crimp
  8. Cut several vents in top pie crust
  9. Bake for an additional 20 minutes
