Coleslaw

A tangy and creamy coleslaw.

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • shredded cabbage
  • shredded carrot

Dressing

  • mayonnaise
  • white wine vinegar
  • sugar
  • milk
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

  1. Combine cabbage and carrot
  2. Combine dressing ingredients
  3. Pour dressing over vegetables, and mix well
  4. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, stirring salad occasionally
