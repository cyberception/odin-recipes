Mayonnaise

A wonderfully rich mayonnaise with a nice zip.

Equipment

  • immersion blender
  • tall container

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg, room temperature
  • 1 tsp dijion mustard
  • 1 tbsp white balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/8th tsp msg

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients into tall container, oil last
  2. Place immersion blender at the bottom of container
  3. Begin blending, and as mixture thickens and turns white, move blender up and down slightly to thoroughly combine the mayonnaise
